>     LionBot

LionBot is a Facebook Messenger bot built to help Columbia students find their way around campus and NYC. It covers everything from small talk to food recommendations to memes! Built with Python, BeautifulSoup for web scraping, and API.ai. Won "Best Use of Journalism" at Columbia DevFest 2017. Message The Lion to test it out!

>     Bespoke

Bespoke was a project created at Capital One's Software Engineering Summit. It was a customizable wearable credit card using NFC technology, complemented with a mobile app to scan and use the credit card and a web app to simulate bank transactions using Capital One's Nessie API. Won 1st Place at the Capital One Hackathon.
